Chritain Faith & Mental Health

Dear Beloved in Christ,

We are excited to invite you to our upcoming program, Christian Faith & Mental Health, where we will explore the vital connection between spiritual well-being and mental health. Join us as we delve into how the teachings of Christ can bring healing, strength, and peace in times of mental distress. Together, we will discuss ways to support one another in our faith journeys and mental health struggles.

This program promises to be a time of reflection, learning, and fellowship. We hope you will come ready to engage, share, and grow as we seek God’s guidance on these important topics. We look forward to seeing you there and experiencing God’s love and healing in our lives.
